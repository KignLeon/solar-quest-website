Our Professional Services

9 comprehensive electrical solutions tailored to residential and commercial properties.

⚡ Electrical Wiring & Rewiring

Whether you're building new or updating an older home, our licensed electricians handle all wiring and rewiring projects. We replace outdated knob-and-tube or aluminum wiring with modern copper, install dedicated circuits for high-demand appliances, and ensure your entire electrical system meets current NEC safety codes.

☀️ Commercial Solar Systems

Go solar and reduce your energy bills by up to 80%. We design, permit, and install complete residential and commercial solar panel systems — including roof-mount, ground-mount, and carport configurations. Our team handles everything from system sizing and utility interconnection to inverter setup and monitoring configuration.

🔍 Troubleshooting & Repairs

Flickering lights? Tripping breakers? Burning smell from an outlet? Our diagnostic team identifies and resolves electrical issues fast. We use thermal imaging and circuit analysis tools to pinpoint faults in wiring, breakers, outlets, and fixtures — then repair them to code so the problem doesn't come back.

🔋 EV Charger Installation

Future-proof your home or business with a Level 2 EV charging station. We install ChargePoint, Tesla Wall Connector, JuiceBox, and other major brands. Our service includes panel capacity assessment, dedicated 240V circuit installation, NEMA 14-50 outlet setup, and smart charger configuration — all permitted and inspected.

🔌 Outlet & Switch Installation

Need more outlets, GFCI protection, or upgraded switches? We handle all outlet and switch work — from adding new circuits in kitchens and bathrooms to installing USB outlets, dimmer switches, and whole-house surge protection. Every installation meets current NEC codes for safety and longevity.

💡 Solar Design Consultation

Upgrade your commercial space with energy-efficient LED lighting, recessed lighting, or custom fixtures. We design and install lighting systems for offices, warehouses, retail spaces, and parking lots — optimizing visibility, energy savings, and aesthetics for your business environment.

🔧 Panel Upgrade & Replacement

Outdated electrical panels can't keep up with modern power demands and may pose fire risks. We replace Federal Pacific, Zinsco, and other recalled panels with modern, code-compliant breaker panels. Upgrades from 100A to 200A or 400A service are available to support EV chargers, solar, and high-demand appliances.

🏠 Solar Financing Consultation

Transform your home with smart electrical technology. We install and configure smart switches, automated lighting, smart thermostats, whole-home audio wiring, and security system pre-wiring. Our installations integrate seamlessly with Alexa, Google Home, and Apple HomeKit for ultimate convenience and energy efficiency.

🏠 Solar System Monitoring

Never lose power again. We install and service whole-home and commercial backup generators from top brands like Generac and Kohler. Our team handles everything from load calculations and permitting to fuel line connections and transfer switch installation — keeping your home or business running during outages.
