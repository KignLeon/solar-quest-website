About SolarQuest Power

Our Story

Powering San Diego Homes & Businesses with Solar

SolarQuest Power has been proudly serving the San Diego community for over 15 years, delivering premium solar energy systems to residential and commercial clients. As a C-10 licensed contractor (#992252), we handle every installation ourselves — no subcontractors, ever.

We believe in the transformative power of solar energy. Every system we design is custom-engineered to maximize your savings, using only Tier 1 panel manufacturers like QCell, LG, Kyocera, and Solaria for peak performance and longevity.

Our BPI/HERS certified solar designers take the time to understand your energy usage, roof orientation, and goals — then build a system that delivers real results. Over 1,000 satisfied customers across Southern California trust SolarQuest to power their future.

Our Approach

Clean Energy, Customized for You

At SolarQuest Power, every solar system is designed specifically for your property. We use advanced satellite imaging and on-site assessments to calculate the optimal panel placement, angle, and system size for maximum energy production.

Our commitment doesn't end at installation. We provide ongoing system monitoring, performance guarantees, and responsive customer support so your investment continues to pay dividends for decades.
